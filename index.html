Celestial Harmony

Mana Cost:

Card Type: Enchantment

Rarity: Rare

Text:

"When Celestial Harmony enters the battlefield, tap all creatures.

At the beginning of each player's upkeep, untap all creatures that player controls.

Players can't cast spells during their opponents' turns.

If a player would draw a card except for the first one they draw in each of their draw steps, instead, that player skips that draw and gains 2 life."

Flavor Text:

"In the stillness of harmony, magic falters, and tranquility reigns."
